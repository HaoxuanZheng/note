# 🟦 **UNIT 1 — Polynomial & Rational Essentials**

### **1. End Behavior (Leading Term Test)**
For \(f(x)=a_n x^n\):

- **Even degree, positive aₙ:** up / up  
- **Even degree, negative aₙ:** down / down  
- **Odd degree, positive aₙ:** down / up  
- **Odd degree, negative aₙ:** up / down  

### **2. Zeros & Multiplicity**
- Odd multiplicity → **crosses**  
- Even multiplicity → **touches/bounces**  
- Higher multiplicity → **flatter** at zero  

### **3. Rational Functions**
- **Vertical asymptotes:** denominator = 0 (not cancelled)  
- **Holes:** factor cancels → plug into simplified form  
- **Horizontal asymptote:**  
  - deg(num) < deg(den) → \(y=0\)  
  - deg(num) = deg(den) → ratio of leading coefficients  
- **Slant asymptote:** deg(num) = deg(den)+1 → divide  

### **4. Remainder Theorem**
Remainder of \(p(x)\) ÷ \((x-a)\) is **\(p(a)\)**.

### **5. Inequalities (Sign Chart Method)**
1. Find zeros of numerator & denominator  
2. Mark intervals  
3. Test signs  
4. Exclude denominator zeros

# 🟦 **UNIT 2 — Exponential & Logarithmic Essentials**

### **1. Exponential Forms**
- \(f(t)=ab^t\)  
- \(f(t)=ae^{kt}\)  
- Convert: \(b=e^k\), \(k=\ln b\)

### **2. Growth vs Decay**
- \(b>1\) or \(k>0\) → growth  
- \(0<b<1\) or \(k<0\) → decay  

### **3. Log Rules (Must Memorize)**
- \(\log(ab)=\log a + \log b\)  
- \(\log(a/b)=\log a - \log b\)  
- \(\log(a^k)=k\log a\)  
- Change of base: \(\log_b x = \frac{\ln x}{\ln b}\)

### **4. Solving Exponential/Log Equations**
1. Isolate exponential/log  
2. Take ln  
3. Use log rules  
4. Solve  

### **5. Semi‑Log Linearization**
If \(y=ab^x\), then  
\(\ln y = \ln a + x\ln b\) → **line** on semi‑log plot.

# 🟦 **UNIT 3 — Trig & Polar Essentials**

### **1. Unit Circle (Must Know)**
- \(\sin(\pi/6)=1/2\), \(\cos(\pi/6)=\sqrt3/2\)  
- \(\sin(\pi/4)=\sqrt2/2\), \(\cos(\pi/4)=\sqrt2/2\)  
- \(\sin(\pi/3)=\sqrt3/2\), \(\cos(\pi/3)=1/2\)  
- Quadrant signs:  
  - Q1: + +  
  - Q2: – +  
  - Q3: – –  
  - Q4: + –  

### **2. Sinusoidal Transformations**
For \(y=A\sin(B(x-C))+D\):

- Amplitude = \(|A|\)  
- Period = \(2\pi/|B|\)  
- Phase shift = \(C\)  
- Vertical shift = \(D\)  

### **3. Inverse Trig Ranges**
- \(\arcsin x\): \([- \pi/2, \pi/2]\)  
- \(\arccos x\): \([0, \pi]\)  
- \(\arctan x\): \((- \pi/2, \pi/2)\)

### **4. Polar Conversions**
- \(x=r\cos\theta\)  
- \(y=r\sin\theta\)  
- \(r=\sqrt{x^2+y^2}\)  
- \(\theta=\tan^{-1}(y/x)\) (adjust quadrant)

### **5. Recognizing Polar Graphs**
- \(r=a\cos\theta\): circle right  
- \(r=a\sin\theta\): circle up  
- \(r=a\cos(n\theta)\): rose curve  
  - n odd → n petals  
  - n even → 2n petals

# 🟦 **MCQ TRAPS TO AVOID**

### **1. Domain traps**
- Log of negative  
- Denominator = 0  
- Square root of negative  

### **2. Trig traps**
- Mixing degrees & radians  
- Wrong quadrant for inverse trig  
- Using amplitude instead of vertical shift  

### **3. Rational traps**
- Cancelling incorrectly  
- Forgetting holes  
- Wrong asymptote rule  

### **4. Exponential traps**
- Confusing growth factor vs growth rate  
- Forgetting to isolate before taking ln
